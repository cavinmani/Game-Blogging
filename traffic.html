Traffic Rider

Traffic Rider is an action game, an arcade racing game from the developers of Soner Kara. You find yourself behind the wheel of your own motorcycle. Pump it up, improve its main characteristics, make epic races on the track and complete missions. A first-person camera view, realistic sounds of your iron horse, detailed game graphics will allow you to fully immerse yourself in the road atmosphere.

Game Play:-


Face new unique challenges in awesome new environments, with many different vehicles. Armed with an extensive garage of whacky, self-made vehicles and mastery over the laws of physics, Bill will not rest until he has conquered the highest hills of Climb Canyon.

Bill still likes to take some time off racing to just conquer hills, so the original distance based gameplay is still here to enjoy alongside multiplayer racing!


Route

This mode features the missions of the Traffic Rider game. Go through the road map, complete various tasks-missions. Here are some examples of missions: reach the finish line on time; overtake a car 8 times in 40 seconds; walk 2 kilometers in 50 seconds; make 4 maximum combos in 45 seconds; walk 6 kilometers in 110 seconds and so on and so forth. When completing a mission, you can add time for yourself to complete it by overtaking close to the car. The tasks of the missions are different and in order to successfully complete them, you need to carefully read the conditions.

Map

The further you advance on the map, the more difficult the missions will be. In the later sections of the map, when completing the mission, you will need more expensive, more pumped motorcycles. In the game, today, 2 maps with missions are open.
