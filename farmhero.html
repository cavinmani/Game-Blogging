Farm Heroes

Farm Heroes Saga is a social puzzle game developed by King.com, creators of the super popular Candy Crush Saga. The playing experience offered by this game is very similar to Candy Crush, in that it substitutes candies and sweets for fruit and farm products.

The gameplay is also practically identical: combine fruit of the same type on a gridded game board so that they disappear. As you do this, you will advance on a map made up of more than 100 levels full of strawberries, carrots, apples, and other typical farm goods.

You'll have access to all kinds of different social interactions in Farm Heroes Saga, like competing against your Facebook friends to see who can rack up the most points. You can also send requests for your friends to give you extra lives or choose to share your 'boosters' and power-ups with them.

Farm Heroes Saga is a good puzzle game with colorful graphics. The best part of the game, like with all King.com titles, is the large community of players taking part. You can always find friends to share your fun.

Game Play:-


Match as many Cropsies as you can before running out of moves. The higher the score, the more Magic Beans you gain to help you win the puzzle game. Defend your farm! Solve challenging matching puzzles and, with the help of the farm animals, keep the thief away from your fruit and plants.
