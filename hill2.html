Hill Climb Racing 2

Hill Climb Racing 2 has been downloaded almost 300 million times on the Google Play store alone & is the all-time top grossing racing game for the platform. Combined with its predecessor, Hill Climb Racing, the series has a combined total of over 2 billion downloads. With constant new updates, weekly events, weekly and daily challenges and tonnes of new skins & paint jobs being added all the time, there is always something new and exciting to see and do in HCR2.

Game Play:-


Face new unique challenges in awesome new environments, with many different vehicles. Armed with an extensive garage of whacky, self-made vehicles and mastery over the laws of physics, Bill will not rest until he has conquered the highest hills of Climb Canyon.

Bill still likes to take some time off racing to just conquer hills, so the original distance based gameplay is still here to enjoy alongside multiplayer racing!
